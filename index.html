Our Story

Made with Love

Kothimbir is a beloved Maharashtrian restaurant nestled in the vibrant neighbourhood of Indiranagar, Bengaluru. Named after the humble coriander leaf — a staple of Maharashtrian cuisine — this cosy eatery brings the true flavours of Maharashtra to your plate. With seating for around 10–12 tables, it offers an intimate, homely dining experience that keeps guests coming back for more. Open from morning to late evening, Kothimbir serves everything from hearty breakfasts to satisfying dinners, all prepared with the same warmth and authenticity.

What sets Kothimbir apart is its unwavering commitment to real, honest food. Every dish is cooked from scratch using fresh ingredients, filling the air with irresistible aromas that take you straight to a Maharashtrian home kitchen. The owner and staff create an atmosphere that feels less like a restaurant and more like a family meal. Rated 4 stars by over 530 happy diners, Kothimbir is celebrated for its budget-friendly pricing, authentic flavours, and the kind of homely comfort that is increasingly rare in a bustling city like Bengaluru.

What We Serve

Maharashtrian Breakfast

Start your day with traditional Maharashtrian morning fare — from poha and upma to sabudana khichdi — served fresh and full of flavour on weekends and Mondays from 9:00 AM.

Lunch Thali

Relish a wholesome Maharashtrian thali featuring dal, sabzi, bhakri or rice, and accompaniments — a complete, home-cooked meal that satisfies both the stomach and the soul.

Dinner Service

Wind down your evening with authentic Maharashtrian dinner dishes, available until 11:00 PM every day — perfect for a relaxed, flavourful end to your day.

Authentic Regional Specialties

Enjoy signature dishes like Sol Kadhi, Kothimbir Vadi, Misal Pav, and more — recipes passed down through tradition and prepared without any artificial additives or preservatives.

Dine-In Experience

Experience the warm, cosy ambiance of a small neighbourhood restaurant where the staff treats every guest like family — ideal for intimate meals, catch-ups, and breakfast dates.
